
# Bubble Sort Algorithm in Cairo

## Introduction

Bubble Sort is one of the simplest sorting algorithms. It repeatedly compares adjacent elements and swaps them if they are in the wrong order.

The algorithm continues until the array becomes fully sorted.

---

# How Bubble Sort Works

Example Array:

[5, 2, 9, 1, 3]

First Pass:
- Compare 5 and 2 → swap
- Compare 5 and 9 → no swap
- Compare 9 and 1 → swap
- Compare 9 and 3 → swap

The largest number moves to the end after every pass.

---

# Time Complexity

Best Case: O(n)

Average Case: O(n²)

Worst Case: O(n²)
